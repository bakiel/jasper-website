# JASPER Forms & Templates

**Purpose:** All forms, templates, and client-facing documents
**Platform:** Tally (recommended) or Typeform
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Form Overview

| Form | Purpose | Trigger | Platform |
|------|---------|---------|----------|
| Quick Contact | Initial inquiry | Website contact page | Tally embed |
| Full Intake | Detailed project info | After qualification | Tally (full form) |
| Feedback Form | Draft review | With draft delivery | Tally |
| Project Close | Final feedback | After delivery | Email or Tally |
