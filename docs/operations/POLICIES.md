## 1. Client Acceptance Policy

### 1.1 Minimum Requirements

```
PROJECT CRITERIA
────────────────
☑ Seeking $5M+ in development finance
☑ Targeting a recognised DFI (IFC, AfDB, ADB, IDC, Land Bank, etc.)
☑ In a sector we serve (Agribusiness, Infrastructure, Tech, Manufacturing)
☑ Basic feasibility established (not just an idea)
☑ Timeline of 4+ weeks minimum
☑ Team identified and responsive

CLIENT CRITERIA
───────────────
☑ Prepared to complete detailed intake
☑ Can respond within 48-72 hours during production
☑ Able to pay 50% deposit upfront
☑ Comfortable with async communication
☑ Decision-maker or has authority to proceed
```

### 1.2 Automatic Decline

```
DECLINE WITHOUT DISCUSSION
──────────────────────────
✗ Projects under $5M funding target
✗ Bank loans or traditional lending
✗ Visa or immigration applications
✗ Template requests
✗ "Pay when funded" proposals
✗ Rush jobs under 2 weeks
✗ Sectors we don't serve (real estate, mining, oil/gas, etc.)

DECLINE AFTER ASSESSMENT
────────────────────────
✗ Incomplete intake after 2 reminders
✗ Unrealistic assumptions that can't be defended
✗ Red flag responses in intake
✗ Aggressive negotiation on price
✗ Demanding phone calls as condition
```

### 1.3 Red Flags

```
PROCEED WITH CAUTION
────────────────────
• Vague about funding source or target DFI
• Unwilling to provide basic project information
• Excessive urgency without clear deadline
• Multiple prior failed applications (understand why)
• Team credentials don't match project scale
• Pressure for discounts or payment terms

ADDITIONAL VERIFICATION NEEDED
──────────────────────────────
• Very large projects (>$100M) - verify capacity
• Government involvement - verify authority
• Multiple parties - verify decision-maker
• Prior consultant issues - understand context
```
