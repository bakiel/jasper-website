### Section 6: FAQ (Submission-Specific)

```
H2: Submission Questions

Q: What file formats do you accept?
A: PDF, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), Word (.doc, .docx), PowerPoint (.ppt, .pptx),
   images (.png, .jpg), and ZIP archives. Maximum 50MB per file.

Q: How many files can I upload?
A: Up to 20 files per submission. If you have more, use a ZIP archive.

Q: What if I don't have all the required documents?
A: Submit what you have. Our system will identify missing items and
   tell you exactly what's needed to proceed.

Q: How long until I hear back?
A: You'll receive instant confirmation. Within 48 hours, you'll get
   either a proposal or a list of missing items.

Q: Can I add more documents later?
A: Yes. Reply to your confirmation email with additional documents,
   or submit again using the same project name.

Q: What happens to rejected submissions?
A: If your project doesn't meet our minimum requirements, we'll
   explain why and suggest alternatives. Documents are deleted
   within 30 days.
```
