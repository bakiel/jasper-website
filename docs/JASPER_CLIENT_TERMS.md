## Quick Reference: Red Flags

### Don't Accept Client If:

- [ ] Negotiates price more than once
- [ ] Asks for payment plan
- [ ] "I'll pay when funding comes through"
- [ ] Wants extensive calls before paying
- [ ] "Can you start while I arrange payment?"
- [ ] Vague about what they actually need
- [ ] Has "fired" multiple consultants before
- [ ] Friend/family connection
- [ ] Emotional pressure tactics
- [ ] "I know people who can help your business"

### Response to Red Flags:

> "Thanks for your interest. Based on our conversation, I don't think we're the right fit for your project. I wish you the best in finding a suitable partner."

No explanation needed. Move on.

## Quick Reference: Scope Creep Responses

### "Can you also add X?"

> "That's outside the agreed scope. I can include it for an additional $[AMOUNT]. Let me know if you'd like to proceed."

### "This isn't what I expected"

> "The deliverables match the agreed scope exactly. If you'd like changes, we can discuss a revision round or scope adjustment."

### "Just one small thing..."

> "Happy to help. Small additions are $[X] or can be included in your next revision round."

### "My partner/investor wants changes"

> "No problem. Please consolidate all feedback into one document and we'll address it in your revision round."

## Payment Collection Scripts

### Before Starting Work

> "Thanks for confirming! To begin, please submit the 50% deposit ($[X]) via:
>
> **Crypto (Preferred):**
> USDC/USDT/BTC/ETH to: [wallet address]
>
> **Wise:**
> [Wise details]
>
> **Payoneer:**
> [Payoneer details]
>
> Work begins within 24 hours of payment confirmation."

### Before Releasing Final Files

> "Your package is complete! I've attached a watermarked preview for your review.
>
> To receive the final files (unwatermarked, full access), please submit the remaining balance of $[X] to the same payment details.
>
> Final files released within 24 hours of payment confirmation."

### If Payment is Late

> "Just following up on the outstanding balance of $[X]. Final files are ready and will be released as soon as payment clears.
>
> Please let me know if you have any questions about payment methods."

### If Payment is Very Late (7+ days)

> "I haven't received the final payment for your project. As per our agreement, work remains property of JASPER until full payment.
>
> Please arrange payment within the next 48 hours, or I'll need to consider the project cancelled and the deposit forfeited.
>
> Let me know how you'd like to proceed."

## Crypto Payment Notes

### Preferred Stablecoins
- **USDC** - Best (stable, widely accepted)
- **USDT** - Good (stable, high liquidity)

### Preferred Networks
- ERC-20 (Ethereum) - Higher fees, most trusted
- TRC-20 (Tron) - Lower fees, fast
- Solana - Low fees, fast

### Volatile Crypto
- BTC, ETH accepted but convert immediately
- Quote valid for 24 hours due to volatility
- Client bears exchange rate risk

### Confirmation
- Stablecoins: 1-2 confirmations sufficient
- BTC: Wait for 3 confirmations
- ETH: Wait for 12 confirmations

## File Release Protocol

### Before Full Payment
- PDF: Watermarked "DRAFT - NOT FOR DISTRIBUTION"
- Excel: Limited preview (key sheets only, formulas hidden)
- No source files

### After Full Payment
- PDF: Clean, unwatermarked
- Excel: Full workbook, all formulas visible
- Supporting files: JSON, documentation
- Loom walkthrough (if included)
