## Core Positioning

**What We Do:** Investment-grade financial models for renewable energy, infrastructure, data centres, and agri-industrial projects seeking DFI funding globally

**Core Promise:** Models that pass technical AND financial due diligence

**The Edge:** Most financial modellers understand spreadsheets. We understand how energy systems, infrastructure, and data centres actually operate. We've designed these systems — that's why our models reflect reality.

**What We Deliver:**
- Integrated financial models (up to 35 sheets)
- Professional business plans (LaTeX, custom designed)
- Multi-institution formatting (up to 4 DFI versions)
- Sector-specific modelling (RE, data centres, agri-industrial, climate)

**What We've Actually Designed & Modelled:**
- 1,300 MW renewable complex (solar, wind, biogas, battery storage, smart grid)
- 700 MW data centre with power/cooling economics
- Smart city infrastructure (hydrogen, waste-to-energy, EV networks)
- Multi-DFI agricultural projects (4 institutional packages)

**Who We Serve:** RE developers, infrastructure SPVs, data centre developers, agri-industrial projects seeking IFC, AfDB, ADB, EBRD, IDC, DBSA, GCF, and climate funds globally

**Who We Don't Serve:** Bank loans under $1M, visa applications, template needs, SA SMEs paying R3K-R20K

**Geographic Scope:** Global — wherever DFIs deploy capital. MENA, SEA, LatAm, Africa.

## The Knowledge Moat

**Why we're different:**

Most modellers ask: "What are your assumptions?"
We ask: "Have you considered the heat reuse revenue stream?"

**Systems knowledge embedded in every model:**
- Solar/Wind: Capacity factors, degradation curves, curtailment risk
- Battery Storage: Cycling economics, depth of discharge trade-offs
- Biogas: Feedstock yield curves, digestate revenue
- Data Centres: PUE modelling, cooling load profiles, redundancy costs
- Smart Grid: Load balancing, peak shaving economics
- Heat Reuse: Waste heat valuation, integration economics
- Agriculture: Hydroponic yields, seasonality, school nutrition integration
- Food Processing: Capacity utilisation, cold chain, HACCP compliance costs
- Manufacturing: Working capital cycles, production ramp-up, FMCG distribution

**This isn't a separate service — it's why our models are better.**
