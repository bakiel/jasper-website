\jaspersection{The 28-Sheet Architecture}

\vspace{8pt}

The JASPER 28-Sheet Architecture is our proprietary model structure that organises all project financials into \jasperemph{eight logical categories}. This ensures complete audit trails, balanced financial statements, and professional presentation.

\vspace{20pt}

\begin{center}
\includegraphics[width=0.95\linewidth]{../images/jasper-28-sheet-architecture-colorful.png}
\end{center}

\vspace{16pt}

\jaspersubsection{Key Model Principles}

\vspace{8pt}

\noindent
\begin{minipage}[t]{0.47\linewidth}
    \begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*, itemsep=6pt]
        \item \textbf{One-Way Referencing} --- Each sheet only references sheets above it
        \item \textbf{Single Circular} --- Only Debt $\leftrightarrow$ Interest $\leftrightarrow$ Cash
        \item \textbf{No Hardcoding} --- All inputs in Assumptions sheet
        \item \textbf{Named Ranges} --- Clear formula references
    \end{enumerate}
\end{minipage}%
\hfill%
\begin{minipage}[t]{0.47\linewidth}
    \begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*, itemsep=6pt, start=5]
        \item \textbf{Balance Must Balance} --- Assets = Liabilities + Equity
        \item \textbf{Cash Reconciles} --- Movement equals BS change
        \item \textbf{Full Documentation} --- Every assumption sourced
        \item \textbf{DFI Compliance} --- Meets funder requirements
    \end{enumerate}
\end{minipage}
